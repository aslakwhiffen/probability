
For each $ t \in (-\sqrt{n} t_0, \sqrt{n}t_0)$, we have that

\begin{align*}
    M_{Z_{n} } (t) &= \E e^{t Z_{n}  } \\
		   &= \E e^{t 1/\sqrt{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} X_{j} }\\
		   &= \E \Pi_{j=1}^{n}   e^{t X_{j} /\sqrt{n}}\\
		   &= \Pi_{j=1}^{n} \E e^{t X_{j} /\sqrt{n}}\quad \mbox{independence property} \\
		   &= \left( \E e^{t X_1 /\sqrt{n}}  \right)^{n} \quad \mbox{identically
		   distributed}\\
		   &= \left( M_{X} \left( \frac{t}{\sqrt{n}}  \right)  \right)^{n} 
\end{align*}
