
Let's take the derivative first

\begin{align*}
    f(t) &= e^{-\delta t} M_{Y_{n} } (t) \\
	 &=  e^{-\delta t} \frac{e^{-t/2} }{2^{n} } (1+ e^{t/n} )^{n} \\
    f'(t) &= 1/2^{n} (-(\delta + 1/2)) e^{-(\delta + 1/2)t} (1+ e^{t/n})^{n} \\
	  &+ 1/2^{n} e^{-(\delta + 1/2) t} n (1+ e^{t/n} )^{n-1} (1/n e^{t/n} \\
\end{align*}

We see that $ (1 + e^{t/n} )^{n-1} \neq 0$ and $ e^{-(\delta + 1/2)t} \neq 0$ so equating $ f'(t)$
to zero and dividing on both sides we get

\begin{align*}
    (\delta + 1/2)(1 + e^{t/n} ) &= e^{t/n}\\ 
\end{align*}
which simplifies to 

\[
    e^{t/n} = \frac{1/2 + \delta}{1/2- \delta} 
\] 
giving us 
\[
    t = n \ln \left( \frac{1/2 + \delta}{1/2 - \delta} \right)  
.\]
