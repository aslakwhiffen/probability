
We see that $ p(x,y) \geq 0$ for all $ x, y \in \{ 0,1 \} ^{2} $. Additionally,

\begin{align*}
    \sum_{x,y \in \{ 0,1 \} }^{} p(x,y) &= \alpha(1-\beta) + \alpha \beta + (1-\alpha)(1- \beta) +
    (1-\alpha)\beta\\
					&= \alpha(1 - \beta + \beta) + (1- \alpha)(1- \beta + \beta)
					\\
					&= \alpha(1-0) + (1-\alpha)(1-0)\\
					&= \alpha + 1 - \alpha \\
					&= 1
\end{align*}
Thus the two conditions for proposition 2.14 are satisfied.
