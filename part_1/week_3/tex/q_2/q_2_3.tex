
By lemma 5.13, 
\[
    \P_{X} ((a,b]) = F_{X} (b) - F_{X} (a)    
.\] 

\begin{equation*}
    P_{X} ((a,b]) = 
    \begin{cases}
	1 & \mbox{if} \, b=\pi, \, a = 0\\
	1- \frac{1}{\pi} (a + \sin(a)) & \mbox{if} \, b = \pi, \, a > 0\\
	\frac{1}{\pi} (b + \sin(b)) & \mbox{if} \, \pi > b, \, a = 0\\
	\frac{1}{\pi} (b + \sin(b)) - \frac{1}{\pi} (a + \sin(a)) \quad & \mbox{if} \, \pi>b, \, a>0
    \end{cases}
\end{equation*}
