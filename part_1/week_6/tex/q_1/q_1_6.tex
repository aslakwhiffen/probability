
True. Theorem 8.16 states that the moment generating function $ M_{X} (t)$ is equal to the power
series $ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} 1/n! t^{n} \E X^{n} $ for $ |t| < t_0$ and $ \E X^{n} = M_{X}^{n} (0)$.
If the moment generating function $ M_{X} $ exists then it is $ n$ times differentiable in the
neighbourhood of $ 0$.
