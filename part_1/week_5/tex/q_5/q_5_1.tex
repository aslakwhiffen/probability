
Let's see whether the expectation is defined

\begin{align*}
    \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |x| f_{X} dx &= \int_{0}^{\infty} |x| e^{-x} dx + \int_{-infty}^{0} |x|
    (0) dx \\
					 &= \int_{0}^{\infty}x e^{-x} dx + 0\\
					 &= (0+1) \int_{0}^{\infty} x^{0} e^{-x} dx\\
					 &= -\left[ e^{-x}  \right]_{0}^{\infty} \\
					 &= 1\\
					 &< \infty.
\end{align*}
Thus we see that it is defined and at the same time we found out that $ \E X = 1$.
